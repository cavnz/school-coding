Submit Your Game!

Finished your juicy platformer? Share it with the class!

How to submit:

  1. Save your CodePen project (you'll need a free account)
  2. Copy the URL from your browser (e.g., https://codepen.io/username/pen/xxxxx)
  3. Contact your teacher with:
    • Your name (or team name)
    • Your CodePen URL
    • A brief description of what makes your version special

Your teacher will add your project to this showcase!
